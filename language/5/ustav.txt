<h1 style="text-align: center;">NORMATIVE</h1>
<p><strong>Article 1</strong></p>
<p>GENERAL PROVISIONS</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>1.1. This paper defines the procedure for the organization, operation and liquidation of a public international organization "International Kennel Union" (hereinafter - the ISS (WKU)).</p>
<p>1.2. ISS (WKU) operates in accordance with the Constitution of Ukraine, the laws in force in Ukraine and the states on the territory covered by the activities of the ISS (WKU) and these Articles.</p>
<p>1.3. The official name of the ISS:</p>
<p>full name in Ukrainian - public international body "International Kennel Union";</p>
<p>abbreviation in Ukrainian - NGO "ISS";</p>
<p>the full name in English - World Kennel Union;</p>
<p>abbreviation in English - WKU.</p>
<p>1.4. The official languages ​​of the ISS is Ukrainian, Russian and English.</p>
<p>1.5. ISS (WKU) is an independent, autonomous organization ,, international voluntary non-governmental organization that brings together groups of canine social organizations on a voluntary basis, of common interests to meet the goals and objectives of this Charter, and is not intended to profit from their activities.</p>
<p>1.6. ISS (WKU) operates on the basis of common interests, equality of members, self-government, legality and transparency.</p>
<p>1.7. ISS (WKU) has the status of a public international organization and expand its activities worldwide. ISS (WKU) is working with state authorities, local authorities, public and other organizations, both in Ukraine and abroad on matters contained in the present Charter.</p>
<p>1.8. ISS (WKU) has the right to establish their offices and representative offices, both in Ukraine and abroad as provided by law.</p>
<p>1.9. ISS (WKU) support free exchange of dogs and Cinologiche information among members of the ISS (WKU).</p>
<p>10.1. The decisions of the governing bodies of the ISS (WKU), issued within its competence, are binding on all its members.</p>
<p>11.1. Limit the activities of the ISS (WKU) it can only be set by the Constitution of Ukraine and laws of Ukraine and the Member on the territory covered by the activities of the ISS (WKU).</p>
<p>12.1. ISS (WKU) is subject to legalization and acquire legal personality in accordance with the law, can have their bank accounts (including currency), it has the right to have separate property, and have an independent balance, seal Ukrainian and English languages, stamps, letterheads with its name, its own symbols (especially the flag, the emblem), samples of which are approved by the President of the ISS (WKU). The symbolism of the ISS (WKU) has been registered in the manner prescribed by law.</p>
<p>13.1. Legal address and location of the Presidium of the ISS (WKU): Ukraine, Kiev 03037, Prospect Krasnozvezdny number 7 d \ 2.</p>

<p><strong>Article 2</strong></p>
<p>GOAL AND OBJECTIVES ISS (WKU)</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>2.1. The main purpose of the ISS is to promote the dog in the world, taking the measures livestock, participation in the work of dog experts and the protection of the legitimate interests of the common members of the ISS (WKU).</p>
<p>2.2. The main objectives of the ISS is to promote:</p>
<p>2.2.1. the design and implementation of development programs and the promotion of dog breeding in the world;</p>
<p>2.2 2. improve the system of training of highly qualified specialists in the field of dog breeding for the needs of the (WKU);</p>
<p>2.2.3. improve the quality of cattle dogs ICN members (WKU), in accordance with the standards of the ISS (WKU);</p>
<p>2.2.4. provide organizational and methodological basis for the members of the ISS (WKU) in the development of cynology;</p>
<p>2.2.5. development of cooperation with national and international organizations, signing and monitoring the implementation of agreements on cooperation and joint activities;</p>
<p>2.2.6. mutual recognition of national pedigree (factory) books, pedigree, and the mutual recognition of the names of nurseries;</p>
<p>2.2.7. The research, which are of fundamental importance to the world of the dog world;</p>
<p>2.2.8. the free exchange of scientific information among members of canines ISS (WKU);</p>
<p>2.2.9. unification of national rules on exhibitions, contests and tests (championships);</p>
<p>2.2.10. recognition of new breeds of standards or changes to existing standards races;</p>
<p>2.2.11. mutual recognition of non-governmental organizations of the dog - ISS partners (WKU) the results of the experts' assessment of the phenotypic and genotypic of dogs, securities, sanctions and the results of studies of national public organizations canine members of ISS (WKU );</p>
<p>2.2.12. in the presentation of candidates for the title of expert judges and dog owners, or other members of the public canine experts of national member organizations of the ISS (WKU) for prizes, awards and honorary titles;</p>
<p>2.2.13. development and approval of the calendar of international exhibitions and events organized social groups canine organizations - members of the ISS (WKU);</p>
<p>2.2.14. representation and protection of their legitimate interests and the interests of the members of public organizations, members of the national canine ISS (WKU) institutions and government organizations and the public and to other third parties.</p>

<p><strong>Article 4</strong></p>
<p>MEMBERSHIP ISS (WKU)</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>4.1. ISS (WKU) is based on the collective (associated (contract -Partnership) and full), and the individual membership fees.</p>
<p>4.2. Individual members of the ISS (WKU) may be citizens of Ukraine and foreign citizens residing in the territory of Ukraine, at the age of 16, that accept the provisions of this Constitution.</p>
<p>Collective members of the ISS (WKU) may be groups of foreign public organizations of dog (hereinafter - foreign organizations). Every state, including Ukraine, can be represented in the ISS (WKU)</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;Only a public Kennel.</p>
<p>4.3. Admission to associate membership (contract - partner) ISS in the following order:</p>
<p>4.3.1. The foreign organization must submit a written application addressed to the President of the ISS (WKU) for admission as an associate member (contract - partner) ISS (WKU). The application for admission as an associate member (contract - partner) attached notarized copy of the Charter (regulations), a certified copy of the registration of other documents confirming the status of the organization canine public policy in the State of registration in accordance with the law of that State;</p>
<p>4.3.2. The statement noted that the foreign organization in the activity is committed to respecting the rules of the Constitution, the Rules, the provisions of the ISS, making the governing bodies of the ISS (WKU), which are binding on its members.</p>
<p>4.3.3. Consideration of an application for admission as an associate member of the ISS (WKU) and appropriate action by the President of the ISS (WKU) prior approval of such a decision by the full members of the ISS (WKU).</p>
<p>4.4. Representatives of associate members (contract - members) may participate in the Conference of the ISS (WKU) without voting rights.</p>
<p>4.5. Admission to full membership in ISS (WKU) happens for the decision on the adoption of an associate member (the contract - partner) as a full member of the Conference of the ISS (WKU) direct vote by majority vote of the Conference, upon presentation of an associate member (the contract - a partner) of a corresponding application to the President of ISS (WKU).</p>
<p>4.7. The representatives of full member are eligible to be elected to the governing bodies of the ISS (WKU), as well as commissions ISS (WKU). The representatives of full member required to attend the Conference of the ISS (WKU) and have the right to vote.</p>
<p>4.8. Admission to individual members of the ISS (WKU) in the following order:</p>
<p>4.8.1. An individual who has a desire to participate in the activities of the ISS (WKU) and meets the conditions of the claim. 4.2. this Charter, applies to the governing body of the local representative offices of ISS (WKU);</p>
<p>4.8.2. Consideration of an application for admission to individual members of the ISS (WKU) and appropriate action by the Board of Directors of the local organization of the ISS (WKU), in which it was submitted after the prior approval of this decision by the President of ISS (WKU). After the adoption of relevant decisions of the applicant becomes a full member of the individual ISS (WKU).</p>
<p>4.9. Receiving an honorary member of the ISS (WKU) is by decision of the Presidium of the ISS (WKU), in accordance with the Charter and internal regulations of the ISS (WKU).</p>
<p>4.10. In case of violation of a member of the ISS (WKU) the rules of the constitution, rules, regulations of the ISS (WKU), the decisions of the organs of the ISS (WKU), the Commissions ISS (WKU) decision of the Conference may be required to correct violations at a certain date, as well as other applicable sanctions, including a decision on the termination or suspension of membership in the ISS (WKU).</p>
<p>4.11. The arrangement of members of the ISS (WKU) may occur voluntarily or involuntarily.</p>
<p>12.4. The voluntary participation of the ISS (WKU) terminated at will by applying to the President of the ISS (WKU) demand for voluntary withdrawal of members of the ISS (WKU), and for the collective - by sending their application and decision of the authorized member of the collective the voluntary withdrawal of members ISS (WKU).</p>
<p>4.13. The compulsory membership of the ISS (WKU) terminated by a decision of the Conference of the ISS by a majority with direct voting of the Conference.</p>
<p>A member of the ISS can be forcibly expelled from the ISS by:</p>
<p>- A serious violation of the provisions of the Statute of the ISS (WKU), the provisions of the offices, branches of ISS (WKU), or the law;</p>
<p>- They cause the ISS (WKU) their material assets or moral damages;</p>
<p>- Failure to follow the decisions of the governing bodies of the ISS (WKU), or a representative office, branch of the ISS (WKU);</p>
<p>- Breach of ethics and morality generally accepted.</p>
<p>4.14. The arrangement of members of the ISS (WKU) (voluntarily and involuntarily) involves the simultaneous exclusion of the person from the entry into force of the ISS, where she was elected or appointed.</p>

<p><strong>Article 6</strong></p>
<p>STRUCTURE AND BODIES OF ISS (WKU)</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>6.1. The supreme body of the ISS (WKU) is the Conference, which meets when necessary, but not less than once every seven (7) years.</p>
<p>6.2. The Conference is considered valid if it contains more than 2/3 of the elected delegates (of the participants).</p>
<p>6.3. Special conference convened at the initiative of the President of the ISS (WKU), the Presidium of the ISS (WKU), the Audit Commission, or 2/3 of the Conference of Delegates of the ISS (WKU).</p>
<p>6.4. Powers of the participants confirmed the original protocols of general meetings and decisions of local authorities cells collective members of the ISS (WKU).</p>
<p>6.5. The competence of the Conference are:</p>
<p>6.5.1. Determination of the main activities of the ISS (WKU);</p>
<p>6.5.2. The adoption of the Charter of the ISS (WKU), the introduction of amendments and additions;</p>
<p>6.5.3. The election for a period of seven (7) years of the President of ISS (WKU), the Chairman and members of the Audit Committee of the ISS (WKU);</p>
<p>6.5.4. Hearing reports of the President, the Bureau and the Audit Commission of the ISS (WKU);</p>
<p>6.5.5. The decision of reorganization or liquidation of the ISS (WKU);</p>
<p>6.5.6. The exercise of the right to property and the means of ISS (WKU);</p>
<p>6.5.7. The adoption of other decisions within the competence of the Conference;</p>
<p>6.5.8. Review and resolution of any other issues of ISS (WKU).</p>
<p>6.6. Decision of the Conference of the reorganization or liquidation of the ISS (WKU), the election of the President of the ISS (WKU), the Chairman and members of the Audit Commission, approval of the Charter of the ISS (WKU), the introduction of changes and additions They are made 3/4 vote of the conference. Decisions on other questions are by simple majority of the conference.</p>
<p>6.7. Vote at the Conference can be both secret and open.</p>
<p>6.8. The governing body of the ISS (WKU) between the Presidium of the conference is chaired by the President of the ISS (WKU), in the absence of the President of the ISS (WKU) - Vice President, Executive Director, or the Executive Secretary on the written instructions of the President . In case of failure by the President of the ISS (WKU) to fulfill its obligations under the relevant written instructions of his duties is the Vice President, Managing Director or Executive Secretary before the convening of the conference.</p>
<p>6.9. The Bureau of the ISS are ex officio President, two Vice-President, Executive Secretary, Executive Director, the leaders of the members of the ISS (WKU) and all members of the Auditing Committee.</p>
<p>6.10. The meeting of the Presidium of the ISS (WKU) must, if necessary, but not less than once every five (5) years.</p>
<p>6.11. The meeting of the Presidium may decide if it is attended by more than 3/4 of the members of the Presidium.</p>
<p>6.12. The decision of the Presidium of the ISS (WKU) by a simple majority of votes of the members of the Presidium.</p>
<p>6.13. President of the ISS (WKU) is a person who is head of the Office of the ICC (WKU), and performs the following functions:</p>
<p>6.13.1. It ensures the implementation of decisions of the Conference of the Presidium of the ISS (WKU);</p>

<p>6.13.3. Ensure compliance with the Charter of the ISS (WKU) in his practice;</p>
<p>6.13.4. It has properties and means of ISS (WKU) on the basis of the decisions of the Bureau performs financial operations, organize the keeping of financial records of the ISS (WKU), the opening and closing of the accounts of the bank ISS (WKU);</p>
<p>6.13.5. It represents the interests of the ISS (WKU) to organizations, institutions and national and international organizations;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>6.13.6. Signs official documentation ISS (WKU) and has the right of first signature bank and other financial documents;</p>
<p>6.13.7. Create a commission ISS (WKU), appoint the chairmen of these committees and determines the degree of responsibility of the Commission President of the ISS (WKU), making the division of responsibilities between them;</p>
<p>6.13.8. Approve the structure of the ISS (WKU), appoint and dismiss the Vice - President, Secretary, Committee Chairmen, Executive Director, the heads of departments of management, and staff of the ISS (WKU);</p>
<p>6.13.9. Responsible for the financial performance, the rational use of material and financial resources;</p>
<p>13.06.10. Organizes international activities of the ISS (WKU) in the manner prescribed, enter into contracts, agreements and contracts with organizations canine foreign public and other non-governmental organizations;</p>
<p>13.06.11. It solves other issues of the current activities of the ISS (WKU).</p>
<p>6.14. The Presidium of the ISS (WKU):</p>
